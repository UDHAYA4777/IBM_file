Welcome to Career Guidance and Interview Preparation! If you’re here, you’re probably preparing for a career as a data scientist. You’ve developed your skills and studied the information, and you’re ready to go…but how will you get started with finding a job? The modern job market can be daunting, and it moves fast.

This course is designed to enable you to look for a great role. It will help you understand the real-world work of data scientists and their place in the data ecosystem, as well as some opportunities and options for career development that you can consider. It will give you practical techniques for creating essential job-seeking materials such as a resume and a portfolio, including how to set them up and what to include in them. You’ll also learn how to create auxiliary tools like a cover letter and an elevator pitch.

After you’ve developed your job-seeking tools, you’ll learn how to enter the job search. You will learn how to find and assess prospective job positions, apply to them, and lay the groundwork for interviewing. You’ll see different types of interviews and how to prepare for them. You’ll also get inside tips and steps you can use to perform professionally and effectively at interviews. After you complete this course, you’ll be ready to look for – and get – a job you’ll love.
